Chronic Hypertension in Pregnancy

  • Management
    • Initiate/titrate medical therapy
    • Antenatal fetal surveillance
    • When to deliver
  • References
  • 140/90.4
  • Growth ultrasound 3rd trimester.2
  • Initiate antenatal fetal surveillance:
    • Controlled on medications: 32 0/7 weeks, 1x/weekly.3
    • Poor control or other risk factors: at diagnosis, individualize frequency.3
  • Chronic hypertension not requiring medication:1,2
    • 38 0/7 - 39 6/7 weeks.1,2
    • Preferably not past 39 0/7 weeks.1,2
  • Chronic hypertension controlled with medication:1,2
    • 37 0/7 - 39 6/7 weeks.1,2
    • Preferably not past 39 0/7 weeks.1,2
  • Chronic hypertension difficult to control with medications:1
    • 36 0/7 - 37 6/7 weeks.1,2
  • Chronic hypertension with superimposed preeclampsia without severe features:2
    • 37 0/7 weeks.2
  • Stable chronic hypertension with superimposed preeclampsia with severe features:1,2
    • 34 0/7 weeks.1,2
  • Unstable chronic hypertension with superimposed preeclampsia with severe features:1,2
    • After stabilization of mother.1,2
