Low-Dose Aspirin Preeclampsia Prophylaxis

Algorithm start

High Risk Factors1

  • Previous preeclampsia.
  • Multifetal pregnancy.
  • Chronic hypertension.
  • Diabetes type 1 or 2.
  • Renal disease.
  • Autoimmune disease (SLE,APS).

Algorithm end

  • Oseltamivir (Tamiflu)
    • 75 mg orally once daily for 7–10 days.1
  • Zanamivir (Relenza)
    • 10 mg (two 5-mg inhalations) once daily for 7 days.1
  1. Low-dose aspirin use during pregnancy. ACOG Committee Opinion No. 743. American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists. Obstet Gynecol 2018;132:e44–52.
