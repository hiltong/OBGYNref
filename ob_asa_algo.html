Low-Dose Aspirin Preeclampsia Prophylaxis

Algorithm start

High Risk Factors1

  • Previous preeclampsia.
  • Multifetal pregnancy.
  • Chronic hypertension.
  • Diabetes type 1 or 2.
  • Renal disease.
  • Autoimmune disease (SLE,APS).

Algorithm in progress

Moderate Risk Factors1

  • ≥ 35 years of age.
  • Nulliparity.
  • BMI > 30.
  • Family histroy preeclampsia (mother or sister).
  • Sociodemographic factors (Africian american race, low socioeconomic status).
  • Autoimmune disease.
  • Personal history (e.g. SGA, previous adverse pregnancy outcome, > 10 year pregnancy interval).
