Hereditary Cancer Syndromes Related to Gyn Cancers

  1. Hereditary Breast and Ovarian Cancer Syndrome
    • Breast, ovarian, pancreatic, prostate, melanoma1
  2. Lynch Syndrome
    • Ovarian, endometrial, colon, gastric, biliary, pancreatic, renal pelvis, ureteral, glioblastoma1
  3. Li–Fraumeni Syndrome
    • Breast, colon, sarcomas, brain, adrenocortical1
  4. Cowden Syndrome
    • Breast, endometrial, colon, benign mucocutaneous lesions, thyroid, gastrointestinal hamartomas1
  5. Peutz-Jeghers Syndrome
    • Breast, ovarian, colon, cervical adenoma malignum, gastrointestinal hamartomas, pancreatic, gastric, small bowel1
  6. Hereditary Diffuse Gastric Cancer
    • Breast, gastric, colorectal1
