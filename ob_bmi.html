Body Mass Index (BMI)

Weight in kilograms divided by height in meters squared (kg/m2).

  • Underweight
    • BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight
    • BMI 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight
    • BMI 25.0 - 29.9
  • Obsesity class I
    • BMI 30.0 - 34.9
  • Obsesity class II
    • BMI 35.0 - 39.9
  • Obsesity class III
    • BMI ≥ 40
