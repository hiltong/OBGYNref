Systemic Lupus Erythematosus in Pregnancy

Management

  • Uncomplicated: 32 0/7 weeks, 1x/weekly.1
  • Complicated: at diagnosis, individualize frequency.1

Complicated: active lupus nephritis, lupus flare, antiphospholipid antibodies with history of fetal loss, anti-Ro/SSA or anti-La/SSB antibodies, thrombosis.1
