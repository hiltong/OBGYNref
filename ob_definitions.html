OB Definitons

  • Late preterm: 34 0/7 -36 6/7 weeks.4
  • Early term: 37 0/7 -38 6/7 weeks.1
  • Full term: 39 0/7 - 40 6/7 weeks.1
  • Late term: 41 0/7 - 41 6/7 weeks.1
  • Postterm: ≥ 42 0/7 weeks.1

    • Classic term definition subdivided because the historically defined 5 week term period from 37 - 42 weeks did not represent uniform neonatal outcomes.1

  • Fetal death: ≥ 20 0/7 weeks.2
    • Early fetal death: 20 0/7 - 27 6/7 weeks.2
    • Late fetal death: ≥28 0/7 weeks.2
  • Fetal deaths in a year / Total births in a year (fetal deaths + live births) x 1000.2
  • Expressed per 1000 births.2
  • Live birth death 1st year of life (< 365 days).2
  • Infants deaths in a year / Live births in a year x 1000.2
  • Expressed per 1000 live births.2
  • Neonatal death: Infant death < 28 days.2
    • Early neonatal death: Infant death < 7 days.2
    • Late neonatal death: Infant death 7 - 27 days.2
  • Neonatal deaths in a year / Number live births in a year x 1000.2
  • Expressed per 1000 live births.2
  • Postneonatal death: Infant death between 28 and 365 days. 2
  • Fetal deaths + Neonatal deaths.2
  • Grouped secondary to assumption similar causes for loss.2
  • perinatal deaths / total births x 1000.2
  • Expressed per 1000 total births.2
  • Low birth weight < 2500 grams (5 lb 8 oz).3
  • Very low birth weight < 1500 grams (3 lb 5 oz).3
  • Extremely low birth weight < 1000 grams (2 lb 3 oz).3
