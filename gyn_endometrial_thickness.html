Endometrial thickness (transvaginal ultrasound)

  • Distance between echogenic borders perpendicular to longitudinal plane at thickest portion.1
  • Do not include endometrial fluid in measurement.1
  • Initial evaluation postmenopausal bleeding.1
  • Endometrial sampling with insufficient tissue for diagnosis or unable to perform biopsy.1
  • Routine endometrial cancer screening without postmenopausal bleeding.1
  • ≤ 4 mm.1
    • > 99% negative predictive valve for endometrial cancer.1
  • Need further assessment.1
  • Further assessment as rare cases endometrial cancer, especially type II, can have thin endometrium.1
  • Routine assessment not indicated but review of patient's risk factors is.1
