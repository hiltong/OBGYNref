Abnormal Uterine Bleeding Classification

  • Bleeding Patterns:
    • Heavy menstrual bleeding, instead of menorrhagia (AUB/HMB)1
    • Intermenstrual bleeding, instead of metrorrhagia (AUB/IMB)1
  • Etiologies:
    • Related to uterine structural abnormalities (PALM):1
      • Polyp (AUB-P)1
      • Adenomyosis (AUB-A)1
      • Leiomyoma (AUB-L)1
        • Submucosal leiomyoma (AUB-LSM)1
        • Other leiomyoma (AUB-LO)1
      • Malignancy and hyperplasia (AUB-M)1
    • Unrelated to uterine structural abnormalities (COEIN):xxx
      • Coagulopathy (AUB-C)1
      • Ovulatory dysfunction (AUB-O)1
      • Endometrial (AUB-E)1
      • Iatrogenic (AUB-I)1
      • Not yet classified (AUB-N)1

Pertains to nonpregnant reproductive age women.1

  1. Management of acute abnormal uterine bleeding in nonpregnant reproductive-aged women. Committee Opinion No. 557. American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists. Obstet Gynecol 2013; 121:891–6.
