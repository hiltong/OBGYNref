Postexposure Antiviral Chemoprophylaxis

  • Oseltamivir
    • 75 mg orally once daily for 7–10 days.1
  • Zanamivir
    • 10 mg (two 5-mg inhalations) once daily for 7 days.1
  1. Assessment and treatment of pregnant women with suspected or confirmed influenza. ACOG Committee Opinion No. 753. American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists. Obstet Gynecol 2018;132:e169–73.
Overview
  • Treat presumptively based clinical assessment.1
  • Treat if indciated regardless of vaccination status.1
  • Do not rely on test results to treat.1
