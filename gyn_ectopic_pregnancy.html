Ectopic Pregnancy

Medical Treatment

  • Absolute contraindications:1
    • Allergic methotrexate.1
    • Intrauterine pregnancy.1
    • Breastfeeding.1
    • Immunodeficiency.1
    • Disease in rapidly dividing tissues:1
      • Bone marrow disease (eg significant anemia, leukopenia, or thrombocytopenia).1
      • Pulmonary disease (asthma not contraindication).1
      • GI disease (eg peptic ulcer disease).1
    • Significant hepatic dysfunction (methotrexate heptocyte toxicity).1
    • Significant renal dysfunction (methotrexate cleared by kidneys).1
    • Ruptured ectopic pregnancy/Hemodynamically unstable.1
    • Unreliable follow-up.1
  • Relative contraindications:1
    • Advanced ectopic:1
      • hCG > 5,000 mIU/ml (failure rate: 3.7% < 5000; 14.3+%> 5000).1
      • > 4 cm size.1
      • Cardiac activity.1
    • Rapid growth with hCG increase > 50%/48 hours.1
    • Refuses blood transfusion.1
