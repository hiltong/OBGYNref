Preterm Birth Prevention

Background

  • Interobserver variation of 5–10%. 1
  • Probe in anterior fornix with an empty bladder.1
  • Shortest of 3 measurements taken between internal os and external os. 1
  • Cervical funneling does not increases risk further with a shortened cervical length.1
  • Vaginal micronized progesterone 200 mg daily.1
  • Vaginal progesterone gel 90 mg daily.1
  • Treatment bacterial vaginosis.1
  • Cervical pessary.1
  • Activity restriction.1

Management

  • Visualize cervix at 18 0/7 - 22 6/7 week anatomy scan:1
    • Cervical length < 25 mm:1
      • Offer vaginal progesterone.1
    • Cervical length < 10 mm:1
      • Cerclage of possible benefit.1
  • Consider physical exam indicated cerclage.1
  • Offer progesterone supplementation (either 17-OHPC or vaginal progesterone) starting at 16 0/7 –20 6/7 weeks:1
  • Serial transvaginal ultrasound cervical lengths 16 0/7 - 24 0/7 weeks:
    • Cervical length < 25 mm:1
      • Offer cerclage.1
      • If not on progesterone supplementaion offer cerclage or vaginal progesterone.1
  • Visualize cervix at 18 0/7 - 22 6/7 week anatomy scan.1
  • 17-OHPC not indicated.1
  • Insufficient data to recommend vaginal progesterone.1
  • Insufficient data to recommend ultrasound indicated cerclage.1
  • Consider physical exam indicated cerclage.1
