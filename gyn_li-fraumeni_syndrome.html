Li–Fraumeni Syndrome

  • Germline pathogenic variants in autosomal dominant tumor suppressor gene TP53.1
  • High penetrance with 90% cancer risk by 60 years of age.1
  • Rare1
  • Breast, colon, soft tissue sarcoma, osteosarcoma, brain, adrenocortical carcinoma, leukemia, lymphoma1
  • Most likley multiple family memebers affected since high penetrance.1
  • Hereditary Cancer Syndromes Related to Gyn Cancers
