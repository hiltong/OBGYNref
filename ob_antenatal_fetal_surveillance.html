Antenatal Fetal Surveillance

Amniotic fluid volume

  • Divide uterus into 4 quadrants and add deepest vertical pocket in each quadrant together.2
  • Width of pocket must be ≥ 1 cm.2
  • Normal 5-23 cm.2
  • Deepest vertical pocket of fluid free of cord and fetal parts.2
  • Width of pocket must be ≥ 1 cm.2
  • Normal 2-7 cm.2

Nonstress test (NST)

  • ≥ 32 weeks:
    • Accelerations that peak (do not have to maintain) at least 15 beats/minutes above baseline and last 15 seconds above baseline.1
  • < 32 weeks
    • Accelerations that peak (do not have to maintain) at least 10 beats/minute above baseline and last 10 seconds above baseline.1
  • Reactive:
    • ≥ 2 accelerations in a 20 minute period (may monitor for 40 minutes).1
  • Nonreactive:
    • Lacks the above over a 40 minute period.1
  • Found in as many as 50% NSTs:
    • Nonrepetitive (< 3 in 20 minutes) & brief (< 30 seconds):
      • No increase risk of fetal compromise or c-section.1
    • Repetitive (≥ 3 in 20 minutes):
      • Increase risk nonreassuring tracing in labor and c-section.1
    • Prolonged deceleration (≥ 1 minute):
      • Increase risk nonreassuring tracing in labor, c-section and fetal demise.1

Contraction stress test (CST)

  • ≥ 3 contractions, at least 40 seconds long, in 10 minutes.1
  • Negative:
    • No late or significant variable decelerations.1
  • Positive:
    • Late decelerations with ≥ 50% of contractions (even < 3 contractions in 10 minutes).1
  • Equivocal–suspicious:
    • Intermittent late or significant variable decelerations.1
  • Equivocal:
    • Decelerations with contractions more frequent than every 2 minutes or longer than 90 seconds.1
  • Unsatisfactory:
    • < 3 contractions in 10 minutes or tracing uninterpretable.1

Biophysical profile (BPP)

  • NST:
    • May omit if all 4 ultrasound components normal.1
  • Amniotic fluid volume:
    • MVP > 2 cm.1
  • Fetal breathing:
    • ≥ 1 episodes of ≥ 30 seconds in 30 minutes.1
  • Fetal movement:
    • ≥ 3 discrete body/limb movements in 30 minutes.1
  • Fetal tone:
    • ≥ 1 episodes extension/flexion of extremity or open/close hand.1
  • 8 or 10:
    • Normal.1
  • 6:
    • Equivocal.1
  • ≤ 4:
    • Abnormal.1

    • Oligohydraminos always requires evaluation.1

Modified BPP

  • NST:
    • Indicator of short-term acid-base status.1
  • Amniotic fluid volume:
    • Indicator of long-term uteroplacental function.1
  • Normal if both components normal.1
  • Abnormal if either component abnormal.1

Umbilical artery doppler velocimetry

  • Normal fetal growth high-velocity diastolic flow.1
  • Fetal growth restriction decreased/absent/reversed diastolic flow.1
