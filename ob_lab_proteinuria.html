Obstetric proteinuria

  • ≥ 300 mg/dL of protein in a 24-hour urine collection.
  • ≥ 0.3 (each mg/dl)
  • High false positive & negative compared to 300 mg cutoff/24 urine
    • 1+ ~ 71% false postive
    • 3+ ~ 7% false postive
    • ~ 9% false negative
  • Use 2+ if have to use

Nonselective tubular leak of large mwt proteins (albumin, globulin, transferrin, and hemoglobin).
