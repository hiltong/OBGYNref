. OB/GYN Nuggets

Gonorrhea

  • First line:
    • Ceftriaxone 250 mg IM x 1 + Azithromycin 1 gm po x 1.1
    • Ceftriaxone 250 mg IM x 1 + Doxycycline 100 mg po BID x 7 days.1
  • Second line:
    • Ceftriazone not available:1
      • Cefixime 400 mg po x 1 + Azithromycin 1 gm po x 1.1
      • Cefixime 400 mg po x 1 + Doxycycline 100 mg po BID x 7 days.1
    • Severe penicillin allergy:1
      • Gemifloxacin 320 mg po x 1 + Azithromycin 2 gm po x 1.1
      • Gentamicin 240 mg IM x 1 + Azithromycin 2 gm po x 1.1
      • Gentamicin 240 mg IM x 1 + Doxycycline 100 mg po BID x 7 days.1

Use first line if history of rash if no history of IgE mediated β-lactam allergy (anaphylaxis, Steevens-Johnson syndrome, toxic epidermal necrolysis).1
