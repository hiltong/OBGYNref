Fetal Pyelectasis

  • Measure anterior posterior (AP) renal pelvic diameter in a transverse scan of the fetal kidney.1
  • Normal
    • < 4 mm at any GA.1
  • Abnormal (criteria vary in literature)
    • ≥ 4 mm in 2nd trimester.1,2,3
    • ≥ 7 mm in 3rd trimester.1,2
  1. Soft marker Trisomy 21.1
    • 1.5-1.6 likelihood ratio for Trisomy 21 3
    • Offer screening for aneuploidy.3
  2. Possible indicator of renal obstruction.1
    • Repeat ultrasound 3rd trimester.3
