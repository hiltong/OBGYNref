Macrosomia

  • Large for gestational age1
    • Birth weight ≥ 90th percentile for the gestational age.1
  • Macrosomia1
    • Birth weight ≥ 4,000 g or 4,500 g regardless of gestational age.1
    • 4,000 g ~ 40 week gestational age 90th percentile.1
    • "Suspected macrosomia" better term by prenatal prediction. Accurate diagnosis made by weighing after birth.1
    • Some divide into 3 categories with increasing risk:
      • Category 1: 4,000-4,499 g1
      • Category 2: 4,500-4,999 g1
      • Category 3: ≥5,000 g (associated with increased infant mortality)1
  • ~ 453.6 g in 1 pound
  • 4,000 g ~ 8# 13oz
  • 4,500 g ~ 9# 15oz
  • 5,000 g ~ 11#
