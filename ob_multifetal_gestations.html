Multifetal Gestations

Overview

  • Low-dose aspirin starting 12-28 weeks until delivery.2
  • Any tocolytic agent.2
  • Progesterone.2
  • Cerclage.2
  • Pessary.2
  • Bedrest, hospitalization.2
  • Antenatal corticosteroids < 34 weeks (34 - 36 6/7 weeks not studied). 2,3
  • Magnesium sulfate neuroprotection < 32 weeks.2
  • Tocolysis ≤ 48 hours:2
    • Calcium channel blockers.2
    • NAIDs.2
  • Definition:
    • 20% difference EFW.2
  • Calculate growth discordance ratio:
    • Difference in weight of fetuses divided by weight of largest fetus.2
  • Risk factor if::
    • Fetal growth restriction in one fetus.2

Dichorionic twins

  • Ultrasound:
    • Anatomy scan 18-22 weeks.2
    • Growth ultrasound every 4 weeks after 20 weeks.2
  • Initiate antenatal fetal surveillance:
    • Uncomplicated: 36 0/7 weeks, 1x/weekly.42
    • Complicated: at diagnosis, individualize frequency.4
  • Dichorionic-diamniotic twins (uncomplicated):1
    • 38 0/7 - 38 6/7 weeks.1
  • Dichorionic-diamniotic twins with isolated fetal growth restriction:1
    • 36 0/7 - 37 6/7 weeks.1
  • Dichorionic-diamniotic twins with "concurrent condition":1
    • Individualize.1

Monochorionic-diamniotic twins

  • Ultrasound:
    • Anatomy scan 18-22 weeks.2
    • Fetal echocardiogram 18-22 weeks.2
    • Moniter for TTTS every 2 weeks starting at 16 weeks.2
      • 10-15% risk of twin-to-twin transfusion syndrome. Diagnosed by MVP < 2 cm in one sac and> 8 cm in other sac. 2
  • Initiate antenatal fetal surveillance:
    • Uncomplicated: 32 0/7 weeks, 1x/weekly.4
    • Complicated (ie, TTTS): individualize initiation, individualize frequency.4
  • Monochorionic-diamniotic twins (uncomplicated):1
    • 34 0/7 - 37 6/7 weeks.1
  • Monochorionic-diamniotic twins with isolated fetal growth restriction:1
    • 32 0/7 - 34 6/7 weeks.1

Monochorionic-monoamniotic twins

  • Ultrasound:
    • Anatomy scan 18-22 weeks.2
    • Fetal echocardiogram 18-22 weeks.2
    • Moniter for TTTS every 2 weeks starting at 16 weeks.2
      • 10-15% risk of twin-to-twin transfusion syndrome. Diagnosed by MVP < 2 cm in one sac and> 8 cm in other sac. 2
  • Antenatal fetal surveillance:
    • Individualize: Some hospitalize at 24-28 weeks with daily fetal surveillnace.2
  • Monochorionic-monoamniotic twins:1
    • 32 0/7 - 34 0/7 weeks.1

Triplets & higher order

  • Individualize initiation and frequency.4
  • Triplets and higher order:1
    • Individualize.1
