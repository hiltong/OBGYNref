Vulvovaginal Candidiasis

  • Candida species
    • 90% Candida albicans.1
    • 10% Non-albicans Candida (usually Candida glabrata).1
  • Prepuberty and postmenopausal women not on estrogen.1
  • Recommended:1
    • Microscopy find spores, pseuhyphae or hyphae (sensitivity 50-70% for C albicans).1
    • Yeast culture.1
      • Useful to detect non C albicans that are to hard to detect by microscopy (C glabrata has blastospores instead of pseudohyphae)1
  • Alternative:1
    • FDA approved commercial tests.1
      • Can also detect bacterial vaginosis and candidiasis.1
  • 3.5 - 4.5:
    • Reproductive years secondary to estrogen stimulating glycogen in vaginal cells that casues colonization with lactobacillus whcih produces lactic acid.1
    • Vulvovaginal candidiasis.1
  • > 4.5:
    • Prepuberty and postmenopausal (not using estrogen).1
    • Bacterial vaginosis.1
    • Trichomoniasis.1
  • Uncomplicated vulvovaginal candidiasis (requires all 4):1
    1. Episodes infrequent.1
    2. Symptoms/findings mild-moderate.1
    3. Candida albicans (suspected or proven)1
    4. Non-immunocompromized1
  • Complicated vulvovaginal candidiasis(requires any of the 4):1
    1. Episodes recurrent (≥4 per year).1
    2. Symptoms/findings severe.1
    3. Non-albicans Candida(suspected or proven)1
    4. Immunocompromized (diabetes, HIV, corticosteroids, immunosuppresive therapy, debilitation).1
  • Intravgainal azole creams/suppositories.1
  • Fluconazole 150 mg orally x 1.1
    • Document with yeast culture.1
    • Supressive therapy after initial treatment: 1
      • Fluconazole 150 mg weekly x 6 months.1
      • Clotrimazole 500 mg vaginal suppository weekly x 6months.1
      • Clotrimazole 200 mg vaginal suppository twice weekly x 6months.1
  • Intravaginal azole x 10-14 days.1
  • Fluconazole 150 mg every 3 days (days 1,4,7) x 2-3 doses.1
  • Less likely to respond to vaginal azoles or oral fluconazole.1
  • Vaginal boric acid 600 mg capsules for at least 14 days.1
    • Warn boric acid possibly fatal if ingested.1
  • Flucytosine vaginal cream 5 g nightly x 14 days.1
    • Extremely expensive.1
