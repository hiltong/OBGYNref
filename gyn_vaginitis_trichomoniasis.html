Trichomoniasis

  • Recommended:1
    • NAAT (wet prep sensitivity only 50-60%).1
  • Alternative:1
    • FDA approved commercial tests.1
      • Can also detect bacterial vaginosis and candidiasis.1
    • Culture.1
  • Recommended options:1
    • Metronidazole 500 mg orally BID x 7days.1
      • 7 day course more effective tahn single dose.1
      • No alcohol during and for 24 hours after treatment.1
  • Alternative options:1
    • Tinidazole 2 g orally daily x 1.1
      • More expensive but less GI side effects.1
      • No alcohol during and for 72 hours after treatment.1
  • Suspected metronidiazole resistance:1
    • Low-level in vitro resistance 4-10% but high-level resistance rare.1
    • Rule out nonadherence to medication or reinfection.1
    • Tinidazole 500 mg TID x 7 days.1
    • Tinidazole 500 mg QID x 14 days.1
    • Consider culture to reference lab for sensitivity testing.1

Retest 3 months after treatment secondary to high rate reinfection.1

If allergic metronidiazole refer for desensitization.1

Metronidazole gel not effective for trichomoniasis.1

  • 3.5 - 4.5:
    • Reproductive years secondary to estrogen stimulating glycogen in vaginal cells that casues colonization with lactobacillus whcih produces lactic acid.1
    • Vulvovaginal candidiasis.1
  • > 4.5:
    • Prepuberty and postmenopausal (not using estrogen).1
    • Bacterial vaginosis.1
    • Trichomoniasis.1
