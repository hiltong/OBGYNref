  • Free/Total testosterone:
    • Free/total testosterone > normal adult female values ➞ biochemical hyperandrogenism.1
    • Total testosterone > 200 ng/dL (2 ng/ml) ➞ possibilty of virilizing ovarian tumor: 1
      • Obtain transvaginal ultrasound of ovaries.1
  • 17 alpha-hydroxyprogesterone:
    • Most accurate if obtained fasting morning during the follicular phase.2
    • Morning fasting > 200 ng/dL(2ng/ml) or random > 4 ng/ml is suggestive of nonclassical congenital adrenal hyperplasia due to 21 hydroxylase deficiency: 2
      • Obtain corticotropin(ACTH) stimulation test2
