Placenta accreta

Background

  • 1st/primary c-section (about same as planned vaginal delivery)
    • 0.2% accreta
    • 0.7% hysterectomy
  • 2nd c-section
    • 0.3% accreta
    • 0.4% hysterectomy
  • 3rd c-section
    • 0.6% accreta
    • 0.9% hysterectomy
  • 4th c-section
    • 2.1% accreta
    • 2.4% hysterectomy
  • 5th c-section
    • 2.3% accreta
    • 3.5% hysterectomy
  • ≥ 6 c-section
    • 6.7% accreta
    • 9% hysterectomy
  • 1 prior c-section & placenta previa present
    • 11% accreta
  • 2 prior c-sections & placenta previa present
    • 40% accreta
  • 3 prior c-sections & placenta previa present
    • 61% accreta
  • ≥ 4 prior c-sections & placenta previa present
    • 67% accreta

Management

  • Uncomplicated:2,3
    • 34 0/7 - 35 6/7 weeks.2,3
