Adolescents PCOS

  • 6-15% reproductive age women1
  • Most common cause hyperandrogenism in aldolescents.1
  • Overlap of PCOS symptoms and normal puberty (acne, irregular menses, ovarian ultrasound morphology).1
  • Ultrasound PCOS ovarian morphology in 30-40% adolescents and not indication of having or developing PCOS.1
  • Evaluate longitudinally but don't assign diagnosis in first 2 years of menarche.1
  1. Screening and management of the hyperandrogenic adolescent. ACOG Committee Opinion No. 789. American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists. Obstet Gynecol 2019;134:e106-14.
